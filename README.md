# 📈 TRADER — Trading Journal

A clean, professional trading journal website. No accounts, no subscriptions — just open and trade.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download all 4 files into the **same folder**:
   - `index.html`
   - `style.css`
   - `app.js`
   - `data.js`

2. Open `index.html` in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)

3. That's it — your trades are saved locally in your browser.

## 📋 Features

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Win rate, P&L, best/worst trade, cumulative chart |
| **Log Trade** | Full trade form with live P&L + R:R calculator |
| **Trade Log** | Searchable, filterable table of all trades |
| **Analytics** | Win/Loss donut, P&L by market, quality distribution |
| **Wisdom** | 24 discipline quotes from legendary traders |
| **Export** | Download all trades as CSV for Excel/Sheets |

## 💡 Tips

- Log every trade — good or bad
- Rate your trade quality (1–10) honestly
- Write notes on your emotions and decisions
- Always log your mistakes — patterns will emerge
- Check your Analytics regularly

## 💾 Data Storage

All data is stored in your **browser's localStorage**. To keep your data:
- Don't clear browser data/cache
- Use the **Export CSV** button to back up your trades

## 🌐 Hosting Online (Optional)
